fifth normal form

2024-03-06

a relation schema is in fifth normal form (5NF) with respect to a set of functional, multivalued, and join dependencies if, for every nontrivial join dependency in (that is, implied by ), every is a superkey of .

5nf generalizes 4nf to more than 2 pairs of multivalued dependencies in the key of the original relation.

dealers represent companies, and sell their products the following schema is appropriate:

  • company-dealers (company, dealer)
  • product-dealers (product, dealer)
  • company-products (product, company)
  • sales (product, company, dealer)
